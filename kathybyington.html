Kathy Byington

Senior Vice President for Finance

Basic Info

Salary: $414,845

Kathy manages financial, administrative, and facilities operations at the highest level. She is responsible for how the university generates and spends revenue and she influences and approves budgets, fee increases, and other funding mechanisms.

Directs most non-academic divisions that spend money, including Human Resources, Office of Internal Audit, Police, EH and S, Parking and Transportation, and maintenance, design, and construction.

Bachelor of Science for Boston University (Bioengineering and Biomedical Engineering) - 1983

Master of Business Administration from Northeastern University (1987)

Been in finance/business role since at least 1992

Former Associate Vice President for Business Operations at Yale (2015-2018)

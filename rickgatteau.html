Rick Gatteau

Vice President for Student Affairs and Dean of Students

Basic Info

Salary: $318,197

He is the Vice President for Student Affairs and Dean of Students. Previously he was Interim Vice President and Dean of Students. He is also Associate Provost for Academic Success and currently serves as the Faculty Director of the Higher Education Administration program.

BA in Business Administration from Bucknell University

MA in Higher Education and Student Affairs from University of Vermont.

PhD in Educational Leadership and Administration at Fordham University (Earned PhD in May 2000)

He also teaches some courses for the Higher Education Administration master’s program.

He is supposed to be "responsible for supporting the University’s retention and graduation efforts" but instead he is harming the retention effort by seemingly ignoring financial conditions graduate students are in and pushing unjustified fee increases every single year.

He claimed that because of COVID, the administration is NOT considering reducing the proposed fee increase.

Quotes

At a GSO meeting, when questioned about the fee increase:
[Secretary] Ok. So let me get this clear. What you're saying is that due to the current situation, the administration will not entertain reducing the increases that they're proposing for next fiscal year.
[Gatteau] That… That… That is correct
When asked about lowering upper admins salary instead of increasing the fee:
In terms of union contracts, these are things that are negotiated at the SUNY level. They're not even actually at Stony Brook. They're negotiated at the state level.
He insists that he has no control over his salary. However, management confidential employees like Rick are at-will employees, not under a contract bargained with the state.
